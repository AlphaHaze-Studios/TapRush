# Tap Rush - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 12, 2025

## Data Collection

Tap Rush is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data to provide you with the best gaming experience.

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device:
- **High Score**: Your personal best score
- **Game Settings**: Sound preferences, selected game mode, and active skin
- **Unlocked Skins**: Which color themes you've unlocked through gameplay
- **Daily Challenge Progress**: Your progress on daily challenges

**Important:** All game data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. We do NOT send this data to external servers.

### Data We Do NOT Collect:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage tracking or analytics
- Any data from your device

## Third-Party Services

### Advertisements (Future Implementation):
When AdMob integration is added in future updates, ads may collect data according to Google's advertising policies. You will be notified before this feature is enabled.

### Database (Optional):
The app includes optional database features for future global leaderboards. If implemented, only your:
- Player name (chosen by you)
- Game scores
- Combo achievements

would be stored on our servers. This feature is currently NOT active.

## Data Security

- All local data is stored securely on your device
- No data transmission to external servers in current version
- No account creation or login required

## Children's Privacy

Tap Rush is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Your Rights

You can delete all game data at any time by:
1. Clearing your browser's localStorage
2. Uninstalling the app

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
